DigiTax, one of the five approved and listed vendors by the Kenya Revenue Authority, offering a trader invoicing system has appointed Statsspeak Group Limited, as its go-to-market and onboarding partner. 

Statsspeak will be responsible for market mapping, user education and onboarding of individuals, consultants, SME's, MSME's, corporates and those that make up the 'Kadogo Economy' onto DigiTax - an end-to-end platform that guarantees eTIMS compliance for businesses across the country. 

Digitax is operated by Namiri Technology Limited which is among the KRA approved technology partners supporting with client onboarding onto eTIMS to improve the tax remittance experience for VAT registered and non-VAT registered taxpayers.  

“We are proud to have Statsspeak as our lead on ground partner to work with Kenyans and guide them to a less taxing process by adhering to the Kenya Revenue Authority eTIMS requirements. With their combined experience in business intelligence, customer insights and analytics, they are the right partner to ensure we work with Kenyans to reduce the friction and pain points of tax compliance." said Caine Wanjau, Co-Founder & CEO, Namiri Technology Limited. 

DigiTax offers various modules that remove the hassle of buying or developing Point of Sale systems as well as a seamless API integration for larger companies with existing systems to facilitate system-to-system integrations. The modules include items listing, sales recording with real time transaction status tracking, credit note creation, purchase management all from one single dashboard. 

“Taxes are one of the absolutes of life, as such the process should be intuitive and a source of business growth which is why we are so excited to partner with DigiTax on this journey to make taxes a source of building a better business environment and helping businesses of all sizes scale. Caine has an inspiring vision for building a better relationship between Kenyans and taxes, and we are grateful for the trust and confidence they’ve shown in Statsspeak”, says Washington Ogol, CEO – Statsspeak Group Limited 

Statsspeak has put together a young and agile team of experts led by the Chief Strategy and Operations Officer – Joram Kabach, to collaborate with the Digitax team to drive this Imperative Vision.
